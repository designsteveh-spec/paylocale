# PayLocale

**PayLocale** is an offline-first Android travel utility that shows how to pay at local merchants, decode regional QR payment systems, and calculate foreign-exchange markups — without processing any transactions.

- **Google Play:** [PayLocale on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.stephenheidelberg.paylocale) *(update when live)*

## About the app

PayLocale helps travelers understand local payment methods before they reach the register:

- Country payment guides (offline, bundled data)
- FX calculator with transparent fee ranges
- Optional location detection to pick your current country
- PayLocale Pro: expanded guides, PDF export, and more

**Privacy model:** payment guides stay on your device. The only routine network call is daily FX rates (no accounts, no analytics backend).
